Common Questions

Frequently Asked

Electrical work can feel complicated. Here are answers to what San Diego homeowners ask us most.

How do I know if I need a panel upgrade? +
Signs you may need a panel upgrade: tripping breakers, flickering lights, a 100-amp panel, wanting to add an EV charger or solar, or planning a major home addition. Most San Diego homes built before 1990 should be evaluated. We offer free assessments — call 619-333-6470.
Do you pull permits, and why does that matter? +
Yes — we pull all required permits and coordinate inspections. Unpermitted electrical work can void your homeowner's insurance, create liability in a sale, and is a safety risk. We handle all of it.
What does an EV charger installation involve? +
A Level 2 EV charger requires a dedicated 240V/50A circuit from your panel to the charger location. We assess your panel capacity, run the circuit, install the charger, pull the permit, and coordinate SDG&E interconnection. Typically done in one day. We also assist with rebate paperwork.
Can you add a 30A or 50A RV hookup to my property? +
Yes. We install dedicated 30A (NEMA TT-30) and 50A (NEMA 14-50) RV and trailer hookups at residential properties across San Diego County. We run the circuit, install a weather-rated receptacle, and permit everything to NEC code.
What is the SDG&E ElectroChoice program? +
ElectroChoice is an SDG&E incentive program that helps San Diego homeowners switch from gas to electric appliances with rebates and reduced utility rates. We handle all qualifying installs and coordinate the paperwork.
Are you the same as other "Bradley Electric" companies? +
No. SoCal Bradley Electric is an independent, locally owned company based in San Diego County, California — no affiliation with Bradley Electric businesses in other states. Our California Contractor License number is #1131979, verifiable at cslb.ca.gov.
